PLOT
Mr. Robot follows Elliot, a young programmer who works as a cyber-security engineer by day
and as a vigilante hacker by night. Elliot finds himself at a crossroads when the mysterious
leader of an underground hacker group recruits him to destroy the firm he is paid to protect.
Compelled by his personal beliefs, Elliot struggles to resist the chance to take down the
multinational CEOs he believes are running (and ruining) the world. Eventually, he realizes
that a global conspiracy does exist, but not the one he expected.
